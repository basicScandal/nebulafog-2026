FIRST TIME?

DECODE THE
JARGON

New to hackathons or the AI security space? Here's what the cyberpunk-speak actually means.

HACKATHON

A build event where teams create working projects in a short time. Think of it as a creative sprint: you show up, form a team (or go solo), and ship something by the end of the day.

AI AGENT

Software that uses AI to take actions autonomously. Think: a chatbot that can browse the web, write code, or make decisions without constant human input.

DEMO

A 3-minute presentation where you show what you built. It doesn't have to be polished. Judges want to see what works and understand your approach.

TRACK

A category or theme for your project. Choose one that fits what you want to build. Each track has different judging criteria.

Still have questions? Reach out. We want builders of all experience levels.
